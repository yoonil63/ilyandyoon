Trip

From Seorak Mountain along the east coast to Gyeongju.

Mt. Seorak is a gorgeous mountain and national park, very popular for leaf viewing in fall. We may witness the first colorful leaves of autumn changing, which actually begins early-to-mid October and peaks end of October.

Gyeongju war the capital of Shilla Kingdom for about thousand years, the longest surviving Kingdom in the history of Korea. It is therefore rich with history and ancient relics. Locals literally live with the ancestors since the royal tombs are such an integral part of the whole city.

We hope to enjoy the clear autumn days together.
