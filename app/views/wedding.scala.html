Wedding Hall Fradia

Wedding The marriage will take place in Fradia (www.fradia.co.kr) at Hangang (gang = river) park in Jam-Won Dong between 12:00 and 15:00 on September 23th. The ceremony includes a reception mit lunch and the “Pebek”, a traditional korean part of the wedding ceremony only for the family and the foreigners :)

Dresscode: Since already been asked several times... For the ladies, the dress code will be fairly casual. I'd avoid showing shoulders or chest and try to wear a dress/skirt and look nice and put-together.






Location plan

* Take the subway line 3 (orange line), get off at Jamwon Station and leave via exit number 1. There will be a shuttle bus every 10 minutes, one hour before the ceremony starts, which will bring you to the wedding hall Fradia or take a taxi for a minimum fare (2500 won).

After the wedding

Afterwards we would like to invite you to have a traditional korean dinner at about 18:00 pm onwards. The place is yet unknown. Hopefully we are able to arrange a traditional performance while or after the dinner.
